About Me

I am a Frontend Developer & Web Designer. Combining programming and design skills, I always strive to provide the highest satisfaction for users.


Throughout my career, I have worked with a variety of popular technologies and frameworks such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, TypeScript, I've leveraged these alongside tools like SASS, jQuery and Bootstrap, staying abreast of emerging trends such as Tailwind CSS and frameworks Angular to construct cutting-edge web applications.


Combined with knowledge of design language and tools like Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, Figma, Canva ... This enables me to collaborate effectively with clients, offering optimized design solutions tailored to their needs.


Moreover, using the Git, Bitbucket platform, I efficiently collaborate with colleagues, shortening project completion time quickly and effectively.


If you're seeking to discuss ideas or embark on an exciting project together, feel free to reach out to me.


Thank you for visiting my website!
